Unlike Airbnb, Booking.com, WeChalet, or other online booking platforms, our booking module was designed to benefit both owners and guests.

Low fees for owners

We only charge a 10% commission on each confirmed booking, with no hidden fees.

No fees for guests

Large platforms add an average of 3% in fees for hosts and 14% in service fees for guests, increasing the total cost by 17%.

With us, your guests pay the price shown, with no surcharges or unpleasant surprises.

More control over your rates

You keep 90% of the total booking amount, allowing you to easily adjust your prices according to your objectives, directly in your chalet's pricing section.

By choosing our platform, you maximize your revenue while offering a better experience to your guests.
